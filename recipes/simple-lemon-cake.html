Simple Lemon Cake

Description

This best lemon cake recipe is perfect for picnics and parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Grease a 9-inch square baking pan
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl using electric mixer until light and fluffy
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract until well combined
  5. Sift flour and baking powder together in a separate bowl; add to creamed mixture until incorporated
  6. Pour in milk, lemon zest, and lemon juice; mix until smooth
  7. Spoon batter into the prepared pan
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
